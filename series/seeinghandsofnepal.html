// SEEING HANDS OF NEPAL

The Seeing Hands Clinic is a massage studio located in Thamel, Kathmandu, which is entirely operated by blind masseurs. At this facility, roughly 30 employees are being trained professionally by a British couple living in Nepal and long-experienced local masseurs, who are visually impaired as well. Besides holding classes, they also supervise their apprentices at work by guiding their hand movements. In recent years, the massage studio has experienced a boom. There is another Seeing Hands Clinic in Pokhara, located about 200 km west of Kathmandu. Many similar massage studios have opened all over Nepal.

The situation of the blind community in Nepal is difficult, even in the 21st century. Many blind people spend their lives at home locked up in a dark room for there are few ways to make a living or socialize in the outside world. However, with institutions such as Seeing Hands blind Nepalis have one more opportunity to live a life in which they are not discriminated and do not rely on other people's help
